Cheat sheet

SIM matching
match.iccid_patterns — full ICCID, a prefix ending in *, or "*" for any SIM. Most specific match wins; priority (lower = stronger) breaks ties.
PDP contexts
1–3 entries, unique cids, one marked bearer: true — that one's APN drives the data connection.
Heartbeat placeholders
{iccid} {imei} {imsi} {operator} {signal_rssi} {signal_percent} {ip_address} {hostname} {timestamp} {state} {profile_name} {status} {status_message} — usable in the URL, headers, and body. {status} is connected, degraded (still sent over ethernet/wifi while cellular is down), or fallback_test.
